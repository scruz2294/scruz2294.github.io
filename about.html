A little more about me

In my free time, I love to be active and outdoors. I'll talk about a few of the things I love to do on this page.

UF Club Tennis

I grew up playing tennis with my family--my father as my coach, and my sister and mother as my hitting partners. I've been playing the sport ever since. I am now currently a member of the UF Club Tennis team.

During my time on the team, we've been:

  • Florida Section Champions | 2013, 2014, 2015
  • Fall Invitational Champions | 2013, 2014
  • National Championship Runners-Up | 2013, 2014

Rock Climbing

I recently got involved with rock climbing. Though it's often an overlooked sport, I've fallen in love with it and the physical, mental, and personal challenges it brings. This sport has taught me how to push myself to the limit--and even past it--in order to become a better athlete and person.

Climbing has taken me to:

  • Stone Fort aka "Little Rock City" | Chattanooga, TN
  • Gainesville Rock Gym | Gainesville, FL
  • The Mountain Goat Climbing Gym | Greenville, SC
  • High Point Climbing & Fitness | Chattanooga, TN
  • Inner Peaks Climbing & Fitness | Charlotte, NC
  • Stone Summit Climbing & Fitness | Atlanta, GA
  • Vertical Ventures Climbing Gym | St. Petersburg/Tampa, FL
  • Aiguille Rock Climbing Center | Orlando, FL
  • The Edge Rock Gym | Jacksonville, FL
